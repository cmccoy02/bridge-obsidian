## Review
- Income tax/franchise tax
- LLC vs S Corp vs Partnership vs Corporation
	- No tax benefit to S corp*
	- LLC are significantly easier to operate
- Have to create your own bank account
- Delaware corporation - stable, tax, etc. 
- Exit rights
	- Right of first negotiation (weak right)
	- Right of first refusal (RoFR) (stronger)
	- Right of first offer (RoFO)
	- Tag along rights - force buyer to buy cofounders share as well 
	- Drag along rights - ^^
	- Put right - force you to buy me out 
	- Call right - force you to sell to me whenever I want (good for lazy cofounder)
- Cap table - if you raise money you need to do this and know how/why
	- Ask ChatGPT to create a mock cap table and go through it
