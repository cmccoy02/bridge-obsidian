## Overview
**Business Name** Bridge
**Website:** https://buildwithbridge.ai
**Total Revenue:** $0 

Bridge is a B2B SaaS product built for managing tech debt. Tech debt is the accumulation of suboptimal code, outdated dependencies, and deferred maintenance that slows down development and increases the cost of future changes. AI products such as Claude Code and Cursor have drastically sped up the rate of software development, which has also led to rapid accumulation of technical debt. Bridge aims to solve this growing problem by tracking debt over time to help organizations build faster and safer. 

There are many features I have planned for Bridge, but basic tracking implementation for business leaders and automations for engineers are my top priority at the moment. 

I first started working on Bridge last year, but I took a break for a while before picking it back up about 2 months ago. I am now putting about 15 hours/week into Bridge.
